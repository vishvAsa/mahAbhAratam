## PREFACE

The present research work entitled **GĪTĀRTHASAṄGRAHAVIBHĀGA - an edition with English Translation and Notes** represents the research work completed under the guidance of **DR. S. PADMANABHAN**, Senior Lecturer, Department of Sanskrit, University of Madras for the M.Phil Degree of University of Madras. The edition is based on a single manuscript available at the Adyar Library bearing the accession number 69785, Madras. The manuscript contains eighteen folios and is in Grantha characters.

The Gītārthasaṅgrahavibhāga is a brief commentary on the Gītārthasaṅgraha of Yāmunācārya. The author of the work is Nārāyaṇa Muni, who appears to be the 14th Pontiff of the Sri Ahobila Mutt order.

The present dissertation comprises of three sections consisting of Introduction, Text proper, and Translation with Notes.
