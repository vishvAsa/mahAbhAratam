#### [Listen](http://yourlisten.com/sarathy.thothathri/chathusloki-thaniyan)

I worship [yAmunAchArya](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/alavandhar/) by whose mercy my defects have been removed and I have become an identifiable object, i.e., previously I was like asath (matter) and now I have realized that I am sath (soul) after meditating on the lotus feet of [yAmunAchArya](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/alavandhar/).

— invocatory verse by [srI rAmAnuja](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/emperumanar/) in glorification of [yAmunAchArya](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/alavandhar/)

ALavandhAr ([yAmunAchArya](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/alavandhar/)), the grand son of [srIman nAthamunigaL](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/nathamunigal/), was a renowned scholar in vEdham, vEdhAntham, etc. He laid the foundation for our srIvaishNava sath sampradhAyam by elaborating the core principles of the same, through his various granthams. One such divine grantham is gIthArtha sangraham.

gIthArtha sangraham is a samskritha sthOthra prabandham which brings out the message of bhagavath gIthA through 32 SlOkams. These 32 SlOkams may be classified under the following topics:

- [Objective of gIthA SAsthram – SlOkam
  1](https://githa.koyil.org/index.php/githartha-sangraham-1/)
- [Essence of each shatkam (hexades) – SlOkams 2 to
  4](https://githa.koyil.org/index.php/githartha-sangraham-2/)
- Essence of each adhyAyam (chapter) – SlOkams 5 to 22
  - [Essence of 1st six
    chapters](https://githa.koyil.org/index.php/githartha-sangraham-3/)
  - [Essence of 2nd six
    chapters](https://githa.koyil.org/index.php/githartha-sangraham-4/)
  - [Essence of last six
    chapters](https://githa.koyil.org/index.php/githartha-sangraham-5/)
- [karma, gyAna, bhakthi yOgams explained – SlOkams 23 to
  28](https://githa.koyil.org/index.php/githartha-sangraham-6/)
- [Greatness of gyAni – SlOkams 29 to
  31](https://githa.koyil.org/index.php/githartha-sangraham-7/)
- [Conclusion – SlOkam
  32](https://githa.koyil.org/index.php/githartha-sangraham-8/)

puththUr “sudharsanar” srI u vE krishNaswamy iyengAr has written word-by-word meaning for these slOkams in thamizh. With the help of that we will go through the English translation for each section of this prabandham.
